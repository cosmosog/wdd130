Audience

Our target audience is adventure-seeking individuals and groups aged 18-50, who are looking for a thrilling outdoor experience. They are physically active, enjoy nature, and are seeking a unique and exciting way to challenge themselves. They are likely to be interested in other outdoor activities such as hiking, camping, and kayaking. Scenario Question 1. What is the age range of our target audience? Answer: 18-50 years old. 1. What type of activities are our target audience interested in? Answer: Outdoor activities such as hiking, camping, kayaking, and other adventure sports. 1. What is the primary motivation of our target audience for visiting our website? Answer: To find information and book a water rafting trip or adventure package.
